Courier Management DBMS Project Abstract Courier Management in MySQL based project, demonstrating the functionalities required to run a Courier Service. The User can book a Courier and canview the status for the courier . Courier Management enables a branch manager to view all the couriers and manage the employees under him. This idea of project represents the ‘Courier Service management System’. The system is being used for day to day activities such as maintain employee details,booking a courier,maintain hub details, maintain company details,process data of employees and many other things. This project deals with the ‘Courier Service management System’. The system will be used for day to day activities like out return, company details, hub rates ,booking, non delivery, and pickup centers.Actually It is not easy to do this process manually because it would become very hectic. Hence it is recommended to automate the process by developing the relevant software as the world is moving from manual working to information and technology era where computerization becomes important in all walks of life.
